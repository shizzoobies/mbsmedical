Built for today, designed to grow with you

MBS Medical was created for patients who want more from their healthcare — more consistency, more clarity, and more convenience. We started with telehealth because it removes the friction that keeps most people from getting the care they need. No waiting rooms, no scheduling gaps, no having to repeat yourself to a new provider every visit.

Our current focus is primary care, medically supervised weight loss, and TRT evaluation and monitoring. We are building toward a broader practice — one that expands into in-person care, additional service lines, and deeper patient relationships over time. Whether you are here for a routine visit or to finally address something you have been putting off, MBS Medical is designed to make that easy to do.

What you can expect from MBS Medical

  • A provider who takes time to understand your history
  • Clear next steps after every visit
  • Follow-up care built in — not an afterthought
  • Telehealth that fits around your schedule
  • Honest recommendations based on clinical review
  • A practice designed to grow with you over time
